Cars in one minute is a growing automotive brand based in Kenya. We create content, host events and promote motorsports in Nakuru, Laikipia, and Nyandarua. We aim to build a united car-loving community!

Mission

Our mission is to position Kenya and East Africa as a premier motorsports hub, fostering a vibrant community through organized events, participation, and development initiatives.

Vision

To become a global leader in motorsports, renowned for exceptional events, innovation, and community impact.

Values

  • Consistency – We focus on consistent excellence in every event and activity.
  • Upholding Community – We prioritize and support the well-being, growth, and interests of our community.
  • Innovation – We embrace new ideas to shape the future and keep pushing boundaries to set new standards in the industry.
